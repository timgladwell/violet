Is This You?

Tired of not feeling like yourself — and not feeling heard?

Menopause can quietly change everything — your sleep, your mood, your energy, your confidence. And too often, you're told it's “just part of aging,” dismissed or rushed through appointments, or left to figure it out on your own.

You might be dealing with:

Hot flashes or waking up drenched at night
Trouble sleeping, even when exhausted
Feeling more anxious, irritable, or unlike yourself
Brain fog that makes it hard to focus or keep up
Weight changes that don't make sense
Constant fatigue, no matter how much you rest
Changes in your libido or how you feel in your body
Vaginal dryness or discomfort
Hair thinning or skin changes
Aches and joint pain that weren’t there before

If this sounds familiar, you’re not imagining it — and you’re not alone.

Menopause is complex — and your care should be too. We take the time to understand your full experience and create a plan that actually helps you feel better.

Is This Clinic Right for You?

Who is a good candidate?

Being upfront about who this clinic serves best — so you can make the right choice for your care.

This clinic is a great fit if you…

  • ✓ Are in perimenopause or menopause (typically ages 40–60)
  • ✓ Have symptoms that are affecting how you feel day to day
  • ✓ Are looking for care that looks at the full picture, not just one symptom
  • ✓ Want to explore evidence-based options — hormonal, non-hormonal, or both
  • ✓ Are open to a thoughtful, personalized plan that evolves over time

You may need different or additional support if you have…

  • → Unexplained vaginal bleeding
  • → A history of hormone-sensitive cancers (breast, endometrial, ovarian)
  • → A history of blood clots, stroke, or significant cardiovascular disease
  • → Active liver disease under investigation
  • → Need for urgent in-person care or in-person procedures

Some of these don’t mean you can’t be treated — they may just mean a more tailored approach. We’ll always help connect you with the right support.
