Services

What We Offer

All visits are conducted virtually — so you can access specialized menopause care from anywhere in Ontario.

Core Perimenopause & Menopause Care

Discovery Call 15 min · Complimentary

A brief, no-pressure call for women who want to understand if this approach is the right fit for them.

During this call, we’ll talk through what you’ve been experiencing, answer any questions you have, and help guide you on next steps.

Initial Consultation 60 min · $299

A comprehensive assessment for women experiencing perimenopausal or menopausal symptoms who are looking for a clear, personalized plan.

During this visit, we’ll review your symptoms, medical history, and overall health, and develop an evidence-based care plan that may include lifestyle strategies, supplements, hormone therapy, or non-hormonal options.

Follow-Up Visit 30 min · $150

Ongoing care for patients who have already started treatment and need support, adjustments, or monitoring.

During this visit, we’ll review how you’re feeling, assess your response to treatment, go over any lab results, and adjust your plan as needed.

Care Check-In 15 min · $75

A shorter visit for existing patients who need quick support between scheduled appointments.

This is ideal for new symptoms, questions, or small adjustments — so you don’t have to wait when something changes.

Additional Support

Weight Loss Initial Consultation 30 min · $150

A personalized consultation for women experiencing weight or metabolic changes, particularly during perimenopause and menopause.

During this visit, we’ll review your health history, lifestyle, and contributing factors, and create a practical, sustainable plan tailored to your goals.

Weight Loss Follow-Up 20 min · $99

Ongoing support to help you stay on track with your plan and make adjustments as needed.

We’ll review your progress, address challenges, and refine your approach to keep it realistic and sustainable.

Form Completion Starting at $40

Completion of medical forms or documentation based on time and complexity.

You’ll be informed of any fees in advance, and forms will be completed thoroughly and in a timely manner.
