# Logo Design

  Andrea needs logo design(s) that can be present and consistent across multiple surfaces, such as:

  * website, both browser and mobile
  * website, scaled down (like in the footer or a scaled-down header)
  * favicon
  * brand indicators for message identification
  * email signature
  * documents
  * business cards

  One logo design may not be able to work for all surfaces - multiple logo types (within the same sort of design) is acceptable.

  The logo needs to be inline with the overall brand of Ontario Menopause Clinic - providing a whole-person approach to menopause. For Andrea's clients, this is the intersection of medicine, change, and self-actualization - the brand is soft and feminine, with a healing energy, but reflects Andrea's expertise and capabilities - she is a confident medical professional. This is concrete, actionable help for women during a period of life that women feel often unsupported and lost.

  Final colours for Andrea's business are still TBD. The `main` branch of the project is based on the "Petal & Precision" theme, and an alternative theme "Warm sunset" is in progress in `andrea-15-jun-2026`
