# Website Content Draft

Andrea — this is a draft of what your website will say, organized page by page, section by section. Think of it like a script for the site: what visitors will read, in the order they'll read it.

Everywhere you see **✏️ ANDREA:** is a spot where we need your words. Some spots have starter text you can edit. Others are blank and waiting for you.

Everything else is a suggestion based on what works well on other menopause clinic websites. You can change any of it — nothing is locked in.

**A few tips for writing your content:**

- Write like you're talking to a patient, not writing a textbook. "You" language works better than medical language.
- Keep it short. People scan websites — they don't read every word. A few strong sentences beat a long paragraph.
- Don't worry about making it perfect. Get your ideas down and we'll polish it together.
