Avoid trying to rank for everything on one page. Concentrate on 5–10 closely related primary terms, such as:

Then build additional pages around individual symptoms and treatments.

One of the biggest SEO opportunities

Many menopause websites only have a handful of generic pages. A stronger strategy is to create one in-depth page for each major symptom (sleep, anxiety, brain fog, hot flashes, weight gain, low libido, vaginal dryness, joint pain, etc.) plus pages for each treatment (MHT/HRT, vaginal estrogen, testosterone, Veozah, non-hormonal options). This structure allows your site to rank for dozens of highly specific searches while reinforcing your expertise across the full spectrum of menopause care.
